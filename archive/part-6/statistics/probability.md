### Probability

#### Formulae

p\(A\) + p\(B\) = 1

p\(A\) = 1 - p\(7A\)                  - `(7A is read as Not A)`

p\(A, A\) = p\(A\) \* p\(A\)

```
Conditional Probability :: p(A | B)
Probability of A given B is true

p(C)
p(P | C)
p(P | 7C)

Total Probability
p(P) = p(P | C).p(C) + p(P | 7C).p(7C)
```
